# Suomi Sisu - TL;DR

## The Idea in One Sentence

Build a collective where people are shareholders of the digital tools they use, and AI agents negotiate collectively on their behalf and run cooperative-owned businesses.

## What This Actually Means

**S-Group's model applied to everything and automated by AI.**

- Collective bargaining on steroids: energy, insurance, telecom, subscriptions
- AI agents automatically switch providers, negotiate wholesale pricing, optimize contracts
- 100,000 members negotiating as one entity = massive leverage
- AI agents run cooperative-owned digital businesses (apps, marketplaces, SaaS)
- The "founder" is the collective, the "employees" are autonomous agents

## How It Works

1. **You pay for services you already need** (VPN, storage, email)
2. **You become a shareholder** (osuuskunta member)
3. **Profits come back to you** (ylijaamanpalautus)
4. **AI compounds value** by negotiating better deals and running profitable businesses for members

## Starting Point

**Proof of concept: VPN service**

- EUR 5/month
- Finnish servers, data sovereignty
- Simplest way to prove the model works
- Build trust, establish identity, create governance habits
- Then expand from there

## Why Now

AI makes this possible. A small team can now build tools that previously required hundreds of engineers. The barrier to entry has collapsed.

## The End Game

VPN and storage aren't the product. They're the onboarding funnel. Once you have 100K verified Finnish citizens in a democratic structure with shared infrastructure, you point AI at the question "how do we make money for our members?" — and you let it compound.
